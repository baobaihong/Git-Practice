Aldric was heartbroken and didn't know what to do. He turned to the kingdom's bravest knight, Sir Cedric, for help. Sir Cedric was a skilled warrior and had never failed a mission before.

Sir Cedric set out on a perilous journey to rescue Amara from Zoltar's clutches. He battled fierce monsters, crossed treacherous rivers, and climbed towering mountains to reach Zoltar's lair.
